Hello, I’m Aniruddha my friends and family call me Anni.

Born and raised in Delhi.

I spend most of my day browsing through web, watching TV shows, Movies, playing games or learning how to code.

Right now I’m pursuing my Bachelors in Computer Engineering. Enough with the facts!

I come off as a reclusive person for someone new and try to avoid socialising with new people, though that is something I would like to improve upon including my communication skills which are deficient.

I like the good company of friends and family I have, enjoying every moment spent with them.

I’m a master procrastinator and like to do things my way often paying less attention to what other people say, I have learnt many things that way by just being wrong although that might not be the best practice to learn as I have recently experienced.

The thing that sometimes makes me despise myself is how mediocre I am at everything, I’m good at many things but not great at anything.

I have a lot to learn in life and I’m eager to learn and have one big goal to be a better version of myself every passing moment.

That pretty much sums up how much I know myself, now you know me as well as I know myself.
